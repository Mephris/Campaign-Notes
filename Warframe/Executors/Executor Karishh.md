As said in the [[Grendel]] leverian, Karishh was a surviving Orokin Executor who lorded over the frozen, famine-struck city of Riddha on Europa following the fall of the Orokin Empire.

He is described as a moist, loathsome, and violet-scented brute who possessed twelve grafted digestive sacs, which required specific exo-sac levitators to help carry him. 

While his frail subjects starved and obeyed his edicts in hopes of receiving pre-masticated table scraps, Karishh lived safely within a walled manse where he hosted lavish twelve-course feasts for himself and his gluttonous sycophants. 

During Grendel's assault, Karishh fled the manse shrieking, using his levitators to retreat into the hills and roseate peaks of Riddha. Though the narrator claims not to know his exact fate, it is heavily implied that Grendel caught and consumed him, as the pursuit ended with a single, satisfied belch echoing from the mountains.
