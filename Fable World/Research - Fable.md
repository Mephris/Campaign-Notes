Making **Light the Body** and **Dark the Soul** is a compelling subversion of traditional tropes. In this framework, Light becomes the "Surface" and Darkness becomes the "Depth." 

Here is how you can structure these opposing forces for your worldbuilding:

**The Dark: The Entity of Soul & Art**

Darkness becomes the **Inner World**. It is the force of potential, emotion, and the infinite space within.

- **The Hidden Depth (Dynamic):** Darkness is where the "Soul" lives because it is the only place where imagination can create what isn't there. It is the realm of dreams, memories, and the "I" that exists when the eyes are closed.
- **Fluid Emotion:** The Dark is **Subjective** and **Visceral**. It doesn't care about the laws of physics; it cares about how a moment _feels_. It is "Art" because it is the birthplace of all creativity.
- **The Comfort of the Unknown:** While Light is structured, the Dark is a "warm blanket". It allows for rest, reflection, and the breaking of logic through intuition or magic.

**The Fundamental Opposition**

|The Conflict|Light (The Body / Logic)|Dark (The Soul / Art)|
|---|---|---|
|**Philosophy**|**What IS**: Facts and Matter.|**What COULD BE**: Dreams and Meaning.|
|**Goal**|**Order**: Maintaining the physical structure.|**Chaos/Creation**: Expressing the inner self.|
|**Weakness**|**Stagnation**: Too much light is a desert; nothing grows.|**Dissolution**: Too much dark is a void; nothing holds form.|
|**View of the Other**|Sees Darkness as "Waste" or "Inaccuracy."|Sees Light as "A Cage" or "Blindness."|

**Worldbuilding Hook: The "Shadow" Conflict**

If these are opposing forces, perhaps **Living Beings** are the battleground.

- A person's **Body (Light)** wants them to follow the rules, work the fields, and stay safe in the sun.
- A person's **Soul (Dark)** pulls them toward the night to look at the stars, write poetry, and imagine a world where they aren't just a physical machine.

Would you like to explore how **Magic** works in this world—is it a "Dark" power that lets people rewrite the "Light" laws of the body?
