At the pinnacle of creation stand two siblings, the painting and colour, the sustenance and the flavour, the body and the soul.

[[Grael]] - Body
[[Tuatha]] - Soul

In their debates, they created a world, and in it all creation was born of their arguments.

 [[Grael]] formed rigid earth, defined the flow of winds, rooted the first woods and placed herself onto the center of the skies, so all creation would be shown and uncovered in its light.

As [[Grael]] formed and lighten up the world in her shine, [[Tuatha]] pained their siblings body in blue hues and shifting white misty clouts, to play in the revealing light.

As [[Grael]] first light fell upon the world, and the first root grew into the ground and the [[Aspect of Wilderness, Relgaia|Wildern]] was born, but it was a quiet sterile world of pure white reflecting the shine of it's creator, so did the [[Tuatha]] look upon the inexorable deathly quiet world in pity. So it drink deep of the very ink it painted the skies with, letting out the first colorful song upon the winds, teaching them to howl, water to whisper and roar, ground to grind and scrape, the Inky song polluting the world with wasteful [[Beauty]], giving life meaning beyond reason.
