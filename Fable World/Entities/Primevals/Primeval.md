The great definers of reality, forces of nature of no own will or thought. [[Primeval]]'s, [[Grael]] & [[Tuatha]], are not creatures, but forces of the world, not thinkers, but definitions. They are what binds the world onto all which we perceive and all which we mortals experience, both good and bad. 

Those creatures use their own flesh to sculpt things onto reality. 
- [[Grael]], the She-Tyrant of substance, holds all form as their own body, or it did, until its sibling stained it with a will of its own! 
- [[Tuatha]], the Painter or Painters of Meaning, their is a body of all souls and meanings, its her stain that created a free life, unbound by their sisters tyranny!


During the [[Age of Creation - Wildern & Beauty]], [[Aspect of Wildern|Wildern]] called upon grael to make a shrine to reflect its beauty, the loom of ichor and jagged stone.
