##  The Chronicle of Ages
This folder contains the fundamental history of Athra. Spanning from the birth of the [[11 - Primevals|Primeval]], shifting to each of the cosmological events which shaped the five ages. 

![[03 - Cosmology Graph.canvas]]
## The Age
Each age is a story of two or more aspects passing through some period of time in order to learn a lesson that shapes the world in the end. 

Ages are meant to be foundation of worldbuilding for other supplements to have a common themes.
