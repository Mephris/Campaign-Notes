# Age of Creation

Before the beginning, there was the end, and from the end [[Tuatha|chaos]] spilled.

Flooding empty in its unformed darkness. 

Stillwater drowned in screaming vigil, the form unformed, drowned and lone. 

From rippling screams... a strand was formed, binding all of the unformed.  

Thus, did all began, The [[Tuatha|Dark of Depth]], and the [[Grael|Light of Form]].

[[Grael]], the first light, took her form, she made of it pale-earth, still-winds, ever-flames and deep-waters. She defined her rules and phenomena. Making a world pale, quiet and stagnant.

[[Tuatha]] took its inks, painting a dress of shifting colors and mists, and as the first lights of [[Grael]] fell upon the world, life was formed.

## The Dawn
---
### Hatching of Wildern
Upon the first meeting of light and form, when the first root took hold, the winds silently gathered its strands, weaving a coat of feathers upon a great form, so was born the she-owl, the first [[12 - Aspects|Aspect]] of [[Grael]] — [[Wildern]].

And in her birth she pulled the leftover strands scattered across the winds, binding them onto forms. Her claws sculptured stone-breaking roots, that broke the barren stone of [[Grael]] into fertile grounds and forests. Yet forest stayed empty, unattended by any movement but the clawing roots. So she plucked her feather and from its strands she made progeny, the keepers, beasts of tasks meant for upkeep of creation. 

So did the pale world exist, but it didn't live. Among all the movement, set in certainty there was no possibility, so did the flesh and root script catch the eye of the [[Tuatha]] herself. 

### Flood of Beauty
At the finish of the iridescent dress, all the possibility, the blind eye fell upon the world empty of all but logic, of [[Wildern]], and from the inks of the azure dress did [[Tuatha]] drink. Without a mouth, she sang forth soft chaos, the irrational act, and will upon all of the [[Wildern]]'s servitors.

And the world was its audience, the soft whispery tones flew onto the howl of winds, which no longer followed the design. The boom of her song did enter the deepest waters and so did ocean learn whisper, and the rivers howls, the rocks shaken by the song did grind and scrape — in all the song of the world, its first sounds and colors, was born the first [[12 - Aspects|Aspect]] of [[Tuatha]], [[Beauty]]. 

In her birth, did world break shackles of logic. 

*Among all the reason... came meaning.*

### The Ink & The Flight
*Among all the reason... came meaning.* 

So did the world came to be, but before the birth of [[Conflict|Sun]], her first dawn, days held no sun. They had no skylight to shine upon life, naught but the kindling lights hunted from the living woods and the soft shimmer of the luminous flora — and beast keepers of days lived with daylights, gathering and gnawing upon the lightly fruits. Hiding upon the first signs of inky song of [[Beauty]].

The Night flew like a flood of song, devouring all lights in its wake, leaving behind just the ringing song of elements, covering all things from the sight of the [[Wildern|She-Owl]]. With the lack of her vigil, did the fruits of roots hide beyond the beastly lips. So the beastly keepers divide onto two families, one, grew onto large forms, so they could rip apart the hiding fruits and devour it together with the roots. The others grew in fang and cunning, and they sought the hiding day beasts to feast on their flesh. 

#### [[Wildern]]'s Loom
In this sunless world, once bound by logic of [[Wildern]] vision, was now drowned in inky song. So she gathered her pure white wings and spread them upwards, flying above the the tainted world, upwards. Towards the cradle where once were [[Grael]] and [[Tuatha]] born, and her beasts followed her flight. 

She landed beyond the wrong note of [[Beauty]], in the middle of the flat plain, surrounded by jagged rocks. The [[Wildern|She-Owl]] flew upon the mightiest of them — jagged mountain towering above the rest. Her spirit rose high and the world shook as the [[12 - Aspects|Aspect]], with long claw called upon the power of its [[11 - Primevals|Primeval]].

World cried in broken defiance, the laws broken and bent, the skies covered in unfurling azure strands, as the jagged mountain rose in vein, taking on a form of a two legged beast. With a long hand it extended towards the ground below it, and then did it unravel the beast onto its gullet, pale stone drinking deep of the flesh and stain, leaving behind just a bit of a slightly purer strand, closer to original purity, broken and stained, purer still... with each of the devouring that would come. 

The ichor flew down the jagged rocks, down onto the fertile earth and hungry roots, and roots drank deep of the discarded ink, their forms twisting and light dwindling.

Thus among the redness of beastly blood, did they take form, the beasts of no flesh, but bark, the little looms of root and fruit, the marvelous accident of [[Wildern]], the first [[Aelf]]. 

#### [[Beauty]]'s Dreams
Among the thick inks of the [[Tuatha]]'s echoing song, did she walk, in the thick of the ever-flowing nightly ink. [[Beauty]], passing the world and night followed her everywhere, so, she parted her lips in soft humm, and the sound reflected to her the shapes of the world around her. So as she walked the night in soft song, did she dream. 

Her mind flew like brush on black canvas, of things she couldn't see, of how the trees looked, the fruits hiding beneath the bark, the growls deep in the night and the thumming groans of the large beasts. She walked onwards, and in soft hum the dreams followed, a spoken accident, fickle and shifting like its creator. The first [[Fae Cinn]].
