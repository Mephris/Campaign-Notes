# Death of Soul
---
Whenever a mortal goes through their awakened time, their soul slowly becomes more and more stained by the passing ink and instability of [[Tuatha]]. Due to this, they feel tired and require to stop, during that stop known as [[Death of Soul|Sleep]], their soul passes through the [[40 - The Spirit World|Spirit World]], being given a short, one nightly life, in a body akin to their or wholly different, upon the end of the [[Death of Soul|Sleep]] dying. 

Upon the [[Death of Soul]] in the spirit world, it is cleansed of all the tired ink of [[Tuatha]] and reformed in the physical vessel. 

[[Spirits]] that do not posses physical bodies instead reform in the [[40 - The Spirit World|Spirit World]], in a place they are strongly connected to or random place.
