### Stygian Customization
The customization of Stygian attack (the "Cantrip not cantrip") that will define it damage, range, and if any; additional effects. 

The customization of Stygian form, what is the animal type it takes, can it fly, how bulky is it (the AC). 

#### Stygian "strike"


- Stygian strike is now considered the melee attack, the stygian can now make opportunity attacks using stygian strike attack. 
> [!warning] Oppurtunity attacks
> Enable opportunity attacks to be done by spending the resource in place of reaction. 

### Level 1-3
Whenever you or your stygian makes an attack, the other can use an reaction in to make a follow up attack on the same creature. 

### Level 5
You and your stygian can now take two reactions before the start of your next turn.
