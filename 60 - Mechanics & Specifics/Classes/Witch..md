## Witch

#### Core Design
Witch features will gravitate towards triggering different reactions from the Stygian, an attack from the Witch will cause the Stygian to attack the same enemy. 

The basic idea is to create an weaving gameplay between Stygian and the Witch. 

#### Subclass Design
Witch at its soul is a utility caster martial class, the magic investigator, so 

#### Hit Points
Stygian shares the health pool with the Witch

### Teamwork - (Level 1)
Whenever you or your stygian makes an attack you may expend the resource (dunno what yet, probably some prof. modifier based resource we will see) in order for the other to make an attack of opportunity against the same enemy. 

Whenever you or your stygian miss from the action: attack, the other half can attack for free. Do the solution as per rogue (can be done once per turn, to not allow for multiclass extra attack)

### Ritualist - (Level 2)
Your stygian starts to recall rites and rituals it knew in life. You gain access to the spells listed in the Occult Rite List. As long as any spell from that list is of a level you can cast as a witch, you can cast that spell as a ritual. 

You do not need to know that spell, or expend a spell slot to cast it (as per usual ritual casting rules), but your stygian must be within 5 feet of you for the entire time that you are casting one of your ritual spells.

### Subclass / Witch Coven 
Choose a witch coven, which will describe whom you will follow in path towards full potential.

Your choice grants you features when you choose it at 1st level and again at 3rd, 7th, and 14th level.

### Ability Score Improvement
You gain the Ability Score Improvement feat or another feat of your choice for which you qualify. You gain this feature again at Witch levels 8, 10, 12, and 16.

## Stygian

#### Core Design
Stygian is the react of the Witch, the second body that the player can move around the battlefield with its own action pool. However what stygian does is impacted by the Witch's turn. 

#### Hit Points
Stygian shares the health pool with the Witch

### Turn
Stygian takes its turn immediately after yours.... but lets be for real in reality its gonna be on same turn, the after the turn just for homebrew copy of already established system. 

### Action Economy
Stygian has independent, action, bonus action and reaction. However they are limited in the actions they can take. They are only able to take the actions specified in their Stygian Actions, later on in the feature upgrade and the player choice in the stygian customization. 

#### Stygian Actions
As base stygian actions they can do on their turn:
- Dodge
- Dash
- Search

##### Stygian Actions Feature Upgrade - The level upgrade for utility
Your stygian gains additional actions that they can use. 
- Help
- Study
- Utilize

#### Witch Feature Activated
Witches posses features which allow the Stygian to take the following actions as response, each action might be either interconnected to the feature or we may make it so you just get more with levels.

> [!note] Spending action
> Feature activated actions still require stygian to expand an action or bonus action. 

##### Stygian "strike"
The customization of Stygian attack (the "Cantrip not cantrip") that will define it damage, range, and if any; additional effects. 
- Stygian strike is now considered the melee attack, the stygian can now make opportunity attacks using stygian strike attack. 

### Stygian Customization
The customization of Stygian form, what is the animal type it takes, can it fly, how bulky is it (the AC) and the actions it can take. 

#### Stygian Action Customization
- Disengage - Fast
- Hide - Small
- Influence - Cute

#### Stygian Movement Customization
- Ground - 45ft movement speed
- Aquatic - 30ft movement speed, 30 ft swimming speed,can breathe under water.
- Flying - 15ft movement speed, 30ft flying speed
