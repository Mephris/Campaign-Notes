### Tandem
Whenever you or your stygian makes an attack you may expend the resource (dunno what yet, probably some prof. modifier based resource we will see) in order for the other to make an attack of opportunity against the same enemy. 

Whenever you or your stygian miss from the action: attack, the other half can attack for free. Do the solution as per rogue (can be done once per turn, to not allow for multiclass extra attack)
