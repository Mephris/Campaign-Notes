## Stygian
At level 1, whenever you create your witch you also create your stygian. 
#### Stat-block

| Stat               | Note                                                |
| ------------------ | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Hitpoints**      | Stygian shares their hit point pool with the witch. |
| **Armor Class**    | Stygian AC is equal to 8+ prof. + int.              |
| **Speed**          | Stygian speed is defined by Stygian customizations. |
| **Saving Throws**  | Derivitive from Witch's *ST*                        |
| **Ability Checks** | Derivitive from Witch's *ST*                        |

| Level | Damage Dice |
| ----- | ----------- |
| 1-4   | 1d4         |
| 5-10  | 2d4         |
| 11-16 | 3d4         |
| 16-20 | 4d4         |

#### Turn
Stygian Takes their turn immediately after the Witch's turn. 

#### Action Economy
Stygian has independent action, bonus action and reaction. 

The actions that Stygian can take are limited to the following. 
- Dodge
- Dash
- Search

##### Feature activated Actions
The below actions are not usable unless specified otherwise. 

Witch features focus on triggering the below actions. 

> [!note] Spending action
> Feature activated actions still require stygian to expand an action or bonus action. 

###### Stygian Wisp
Your stygian sends forth a bundle of the energy that crashes against the enemy. 

Your Stygian attacks using your intelligence modifier.  


### Stygian Customization
At level 1 you create your Stygian by taking a single Mind and Flesh feature. 

#### Stygian Mind Customization
- Disengage - Fast
- Hide - Small
- Influence - Cute

#### Stygian Flesh Customization
- Dainty - 45ft movement speed
- Hardy - 30ft movement speed, +1 to AC
- Aquatic - 15ft movement speed, 60ft swimming speed, can breathe under water.
- Flying - 15ft movement speed, 30ft flying speed
