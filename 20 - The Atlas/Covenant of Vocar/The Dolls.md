## Public Information 

### New Product! Dolls!
The [[The Dolls]] are the newest creation made by the [[Lune]] family, quickly taking the higher echelons of power by storm as the newest and best invention. 

[[The Dolls]] are made to be the perfect servants, behaving and with comparable intelligence to mortals. While lacking the defining trait of all mortal creatures... Free Will. 

[[The Dolls]] posses Ego, each one being specifically created by the [[Lune]] family for custom orders and take between few weeks and multiple months to make. 

They are an item you can buy within the Covenant of Vocar. 

### Directives
[[The Dolls]] have directives they have to listen at all times:
- Do not hurt your owner
- Do not kill inteligent creatures
- Obey the command of your owner
and added recently due to mass self mutilation and destruction of the Dolls. 
- Do not end your own existance or break your body unless allowed. 

### Molds!
While it is not sure as to why, the inventors do not make anything but the humanoid dolls and keep their shape smiliar to other races at least vaguely. Some pets have been created however the inteligence of [[The Dolls]] seem to be decided by the form they take. 

## Soul Slavery
When there is will there is a way, and no thing holy ever stopped [[Lune]] family from progress. 

After commercializing [[MTD.pdf]] Plague, they took on different aim, the few Arcane souls that were created by the rapid research were in need of vessels, and with the mana crystals and a bit of artisanship the vessels were made - [[The Dolls]]

### Bound Will 
A willing or an Unwilling, usually the latter, Arcane soul is bound within the vessel, fed by the ever-replenishing mana crystal. 

Once sealed inside the mana core, Arcane Souls go through torture meant to break their minds or at times the souls are just mildly nibbled on by Forget spell. 

Once refurbished, they are let out with few directives made by the process of [[Name]].
- Do not hurt your owner
- Do not kill inteligent creatures, including yourself. 
- Do not remember your refurbishing process
- You are not the real you, even if you are
- Obey the commands of your Lords

The rules binding [[The Dolls]] work only if their form is the same as the ones they have in life. Adjustements to form can be made to change the appearance however beasts must remain beasts and humanoids humanoid. Otherwise the spell binding [[The Dolls]] is broken and they regain freedom. 

#### Freedom
At rare occasions the directives stop working, [[The Dolls]] regain some semblace of memory. 

Fully breaking free often ends in frenzy, feral attacks, lack of inteligence, extreme self mutilation and eventual ~~death~~ disablement.
