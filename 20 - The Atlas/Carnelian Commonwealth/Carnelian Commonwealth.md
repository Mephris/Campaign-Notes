Across the fertile lowlands of [[Lethrai]] stands the nation forged by briefest and most determined race. What once began as a foolish dream of a [[Dakr - Human|Dakr]] merchant has grown into a testament of [[Dakr - Human|Human]] will. 

Once called “wither-blooms” by the elves, humans were seen as little more than fleeting moments in the histories of long-lived races. 

The landscape of Carnelian is shaped by pragmatic ingenuity: rolling plains converted to fields that can feed both citizen and soldier, granaries and barracks in large cities able to hold all surrounding villages, and rivers strategically redirected to serve both commerce and defense. At the borders of the cities stand large wooden watchtowers, with the standard of a ram horned phoenix in the golden fields.

At the heart of this kingdom lies the city bearing the kingdoms name. Once a village in the midst of barbaric lands, now a crimson jewel holding the royal palace, high temples and the Aspect of Eternal War themselves.

At the center of all political and religious struggles lies the [[The Saint Carnelian]], the Aspect of Eternal Conflict made mortal to live and fight together with its brief kin. Standing as a beacon of hope and the catastrophic deterrent to neighboring nations.

When Carnelian’s borders are threatened beyond tolerance, a sight few outsiders have witnessed unfolds—the smoke from a thousand clerical censers darkens the sky, forge-hammers ring without ceasing, and every citizen becomes part of a war machine that has, against all odds, pushed back the borders of immortal kingdoms and carved out a place where the shortest-lived race has built a legacy that grows stronger with each fleeting generation passed.
