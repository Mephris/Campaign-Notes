Among the [[Dakr - Human]] there once was a merchant, who's mind held no equal. He gathered other [[Dakr - Human]] to himself, creating a town in the middle of ashen land where his people could live in peace and respect. 

At first, it was him who spoke and negotiated with many of the [[Aelf]], until the [[3 - Age of Miracles - Life & Ambition|Third Age]], when the conflict between [[Life]] and [[Ambition]] burned bright and he was at the cusp of his life, did [[Conflict]] herself bestow upon him a name. 

> [!quote] They who's [[Death of Body - Passing|Death of Body]] is as the [[Death of Soul|Death of Soul]]

With this name, he burnt down and awakened again in another life, with no concrete memory of the last one but purpose. To keep his Carnelian save. 

### The Name of Phenix 
The name of the [[The Saint Carnelian]] is the only one mortal bearing the name given by [[Conflict]]. At least the only notable one. With their name they have gotten an everlasting flame, to upon [[Death of Body - Passing]], to be cleansed of their form in flames of [[Conflict]] and reformed in their ashes onto another life, another body, already at peak of their form. 

The only thing which remains among the incarnations are the supposed memory, mimicking an extremely talented [[Dakr - Human]] that learns things in instant.
