The [[Móraelf]] kingdom following the philosophy of loss and growth set by [[Candella]]. 

### The title of Tyrian Dreadnaught
Among the [[Kingdom of Tyrian]] there is a single title held by the strongest warrior, title of a Dreadnaught is passed whenever a warrior kills their predecessor or wins the grand melee. 

Dreadnaughts are influential warlords who hold sway in the warrior celibacy of the church of [[Candella]]. 

The first Dreadnaught is [[Candella]], The Dreadnaught of Looms Gullet.
