### What is Abysal Plague? 
During the [[2 - Age of Knowledge - Conflict and Empathy|Age of Knowledge]], whenever [[Life]] was struck down by [[Valka]] and found her existence meaningless. The lifespan around the world started to be cut short by a passing plague symbolized by ink like stains under the skin. 

The [[Abyssal Plague]] ended at the [[3 - Age of Miracles - Life & Ambition#Twilight of an Age|Twilight of the 3rd Age]] whenever [[Life]] emerged from the ocean. However the visage of inky marks upon the skin to this day evokes dread in lives of many long living [[Aelf]].
