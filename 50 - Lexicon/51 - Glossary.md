## [[Grael]]
___
Meaning the physical, matter, the body containing all things. Often Grael is used interchangeably with "Body".  
## [[Tuatha]]
---
It Means the undefined, the meaning, the change and possibility. Often Grael is used interchangeably with "Soul"
## [[Name]]
---
The act of placing a reality reforming bind and blessing or curse upon Body or Soul of the living being.
