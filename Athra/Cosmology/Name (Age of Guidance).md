The rite of naming is the highest importance in the age of Guidance. It is during this moment the child or an accomplished adult is given a scrying from the [[Ishtar]] or one of the [[Guides]] in order to find the best profession or life path one will be proficient in.

To those that accomplished many heroic deeds or has executed their positions to unparalleled perfection. Another name is given, this time enchanted with a blessing of [[Ishtar]] itself.

The Blessings can vary from "He who serves the best dishes" to as in example or Lua "He who shall always rise equal to the challange"

While the name is given by the outside force, the meaning itself changes it's nature as the bearer changes their perception.

A name of "He who always rises equal to the challange" might lose the sight of all challenges and in turn lose the blessing, or the best cook of [[Avalon]] in the tragic accident to his family might give a whole new meaning to "The Vengeance is a Dish best served cold"

Names while powerful are scarce, especially in the [[Age of Adventure]]. However those that bear them have additional perk of being resistant to all signs of [[Entropy]].
