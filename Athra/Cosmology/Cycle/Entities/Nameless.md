An entity within the void, Ishtar, the will of the world that has passed. 

"I am all which is not, all which was and will be again"

She often appears to those lost in the void and souls bound to the house empty. Offering to them a way to ease their pain in her dark realm. 

She is the [[Spectral body]] with an ego, holding within it the realms of [[Lua|Moon]], [[The House of Empty]], and at the border of her body... [[The Koutetuji]].

Wearing a thick black veil showing no face behind it or a way for its wearer to see the world beyond it, a long dress supported by the iron frame in order to hide the thinned body. A thick black satin crumpled into a scarf around the neck. Whenever wind comes to move the fabric of the veil it always moves in such a way to never push against the skin of the face or away from it, suggesting magical item or something worse. 

Her presence often creates an grotesque fascination and awe in those that behold her.
