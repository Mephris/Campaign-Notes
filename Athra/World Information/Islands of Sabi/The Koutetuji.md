During the [[Age of Guidance]] a ship of souls and bodies was sent out thought the [[Athra/Cosmology/Void/Void]] arriving on the [[Age of Adventure]]

The colonists of the ship found their bodies adapting into the new world while the technology itself stopped working due to the [[Cyclic Dispersion Phenomenon]] 

As their technology stopped working the colonists slowly adapted to the new hostile environment where their magics and technologies simply did not work, their martial techniques not used to the new environment as on top of it all their bodies become sickly, extremely volatile to the touch of mana (a genetic [[MTD]]) causing their body to grow long bone cancerous horns on their heads that as it formed, covered itself in a layer of crystal.
