The wild centaur tribes of the Lethrai Plains. 

Their history goes back to the old Bogóir whom they often had territorial squabbles against. However it never became an all out war simply due to lack of unification of both races. 

After the start of [[Carnelian Kingdom|Carnelian]] expansion, the tribes started to slowly unite into large army sized Wild Tribes

Some centaurs life peacefully adapted to the new ways, constantly fighting the prejudice of their Wild Tribe counterpart.![[Centaur.png|https://www.artstation.com/artwork/4NGXwY]]
