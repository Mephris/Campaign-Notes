The last daughter of the [[Draighearth]] family. 

![[Skellye_Drieghearth.png]]

Taught by the servant of the [[Draighearth]] house simply referred to as [[Smithy master]]. Scelia was born in the slum of the old [[Draighearth]] mansion, clawing her way out before any time she could remember in the hands of her brother [[Dwers Grahaim]] as the two were quickly taken in by the [[Smithy master]]. 

There did the young girl grow up, learning the ways of ice and fire magic as well as the rules of [[Cold Smith|Cold smithing]] while her brother entered the military, becoming a squire of the [[Carnelian Order]] before being taken in by the [[Grahain Order]] and bound to a [[Celia Grahaim| Tender Celia of the Grahaim House]].
