A bastard of the Varcró family from a maid and previous King as well as a half-brother of [[Zenrion Varcró]]. 

From young age sent out onto battlefield, he was the most zelous follower of the Phenix, knowing the [[Tenets of Grahaim | Tenets]] and [[Tale of the Firebird]] by heart, taught by his [[Valkyr|grandfather]].

Joining the [[Atgeir Corpse]] he climbed the ranks by surviving the death sentence missions
