The 33rd saint of the [[Aspect of Conflict, Firebird]] reborn after her [[32nd Saint| predecessor]] abandoned [[Carnelian Kingdom| the kingdom of eternal war]]. 

She was born within the Sabi Archipelago on the Demon Island, where she spent most of her lifespan as a cost of evoking the [[Aspectrual body]] before being rescued by the four priests and set of knights of the [[Carnelian Order]].

Later on four local's have been recruited to transport the Saintess onto the ports of the Archipelago so that she could return to homeland she has never seen. 

During the travels the young Saintess seen much of the Archipelago and exibited no talent towards anything else then using a whip or healing, however with severe [[MTD | disease]] she could not use much of her magic. 

During a traversal from a Shadow Market and storing within a pocket dimension, she was spit out without her protectors, causing her to spend next 10 months traveling next to her "Stalker" companion until they finally made it back onto the surface. 

Once there, the party of adventurers reconciled after a long time and started to find a way out of the wreck of a ship they found themselves in, a gate out proved to be an exit from death of starvation onto the [[The House of Empty]] and their host [[Storyteller]]. 

As saintess traveled with the party, one of which unlocked a single shackle on the [[Promise]] hand, they managed to get out with their lifes back close to the port city of the archipelago. 

In there they met with the main force of the Carnelian Division sent to find the saintess. 

It was there that the saintess met:
- [[Arlith Ved'gnim]], a high priest on his way to become a Cardinal of the liberatian wing of the [[Church of the Firebird]].
- [[Scelia Draighearth(Skellye Draighearth)]], a [[Cold Smith]] warrioress tasked with taking care of the weaponry of the division. 
- Henryk, a soldier.

While in the tavern of the town she met a young merchant [[Loriia]] who later was found out to be the last objective to be passed before the party could travel onto Carnelian. 

In the end the party burned the dead corpse of the [[Loriia|hexer]] before traveling for 10 months onto the city of Carnelian.
