# Clan of Grahaim

_“Brother, remember my voice.  
Remember who you were, before the iron,  
before the blood and beast  
I am here. You are still human.”_Tender’s Prayer

Among the Knights of Carnelian stand those who wear hulking suits of armor, towering above common knights. These are the elites of the Carnelian Order, the Clanmates of Grahaim—Beast Blooded Knights clad in Iron Maiden armor, charging onto battlefields not in units and armies but in bonded pairs, tearing through the enemies of the Kingdom.

#### Organization and Training

Clan of Grahaim is an specialized segment of the Carnelian Order, meaning that for the most part how they gain members onto their ranks remains the same. However only few are selected onto Grahaim Selection Known as the Taming.

##### The Taming

During Knights of Carnelian training, the weakest and strongest are selected from among the younglings. Each of the strongest and most aggressive is paired with a weaker teammate, forging bonds that will either save or damn them both.

As time passes and training progresses, the weaker squires learn healing and support techniques, becoming Tenders, while the stronger squires don increasingly heavy armor to endure the coming trials.

The Taming is the night when both either die or become members of the Grahaim Clan. The Iron-Clad squire drinks beast blood, slowly losing control as their Tender attempts to keep them sane, becoming the anchor for a newly made therianthrope.

Those who survive emerge with strength to level armies—humans bearing therianthropic power, encased in Iron Maiden tower armor too heavy for mortal limits and tight enough to prevent the wearer from ever transforming into full beast form, thus maintaining their sanity.

The Tenders continue their sacred duty, becoming lifelong caretakers and managers of their Grahaim knights, bound by oaths stronger than blood.

##### Quick Reaction Troops

All members which completed the training are given the missions of the Grahaim clan as the quick reaction troops. Whenever one of the fronts is being pushed back or a new enemy was found in the territory, Knights of Grahaim are sent forth with the ballistic magic forth onto the location, sent out as living projectiles with spells on them to break the fall at last moment.

Like this Knights of Grahaim are known also as the Angels of War due to their tendency to deploy at last moment, on top of it looking like a help sent from the heavens themselves.

#### Territory

Knights of Grahaim occupy specialized chambers within the main Carnelian compound, featuring reinforced floors for armor weight and ritual chambers for Taming ceremonies. Their Tender workshops contain the specialized tools needed for armor maintenance and emergency restraint protocols.
## Iron Maiden Armor

_Armor (plate), very rare (requires attunement)_

**Armor Class:** 18

**Weight:** 85 lbs

**Properties:** Heavy, Stealth disadvantage

This imposing suit of black iron plate armor towers over normal plate, its joints reinforced with additional plating and internal restraints. The helmet features a narrow visor slit that provides limited visibility but maximum protection. Built-in tubes and maintenance ports allow for external care without removing the armor entirely.

While wearing this armor, you gain the following benefits:

**Heavy Impact.** You have advantage on saving throws against being knocked prone, pushed, or moved against your will.

**Crushing Weight.** When you hit with a melee weapon attack, the attack deals an additional 2 bludgeoning damage.

**Intimidating Presence.** You have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks against creatures that can see you.

**Iron Shell.** You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from ranged attacks.

**Insulated Construction.** The armor’s double-layered design protects you from temperature effects. You have advantage on saving throws against extreme heat and cold, and you take no damage from the _heat metal_ spell, though the armor still becomes hot to the touch for others.

**Restrictive Construction.** While wearing this armor:

- Your speed is reduced by 10 feet (minimum 5 feet).

- You automatically fail Dexterity (Stealth) checks and Strength (Athletics) checks made to swim.

- If you have any shapeshifting abilities (such as from lycanthropy or the _polymorph_ spell), you cannot use them while wearing this armor.

- Donning or doffing this armor requires assistance from another creature and takes 10 minutes.
