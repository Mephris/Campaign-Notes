The humans that in their constant acts stepped further and further from human until they became the void bound beast, kept in this world simply by the last saving gift of the [[Fable World/Entities/Aspects/Aspects of Tuatha/Empathy]]

The Kaithry will try their hardest to not show their monstrous side, the one that trully got them into what they are now, the [[Hedon]] elder.

Feeding on blood and flesh of fearful prey, they enjoy toying with their victims before devouring them mid combat.

The Kryraic body once unleashed stretches its limbs and painfully strains muscles, keeping them regenerating as the body tries to stretch to unhuman lengths.

Jaw bone unlatches from the skull and is kept by the additional muscles developed at its edge and coming from the area of the throat as the throat itself gets covered by the rows of sharp teeth.

While this form can be seen as uncanny mortal form or very slim unhuman almost knight in the armor, they always look like omens of bad luck and clear sign to run away.

[[Kaithry]] that subdue their instincts due to their empathy often forcefully pull their bodies back and remove the throat teeth in order to look normal, however the moment they let their instinct take over the body shifts back to its horrific form.
