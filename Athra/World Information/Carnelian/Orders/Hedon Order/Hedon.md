The first and oldest [[Kryraic]], his image being bound in rock together with his blade [[Unmaker]]. 

During his time he has led many men and women onto the depraved paths with the [[Unmaker]] in hand, only to realize what he has done in a moment too late when he became a [[Kryraic]]

![[Hedon.png]] https://www.artstation.com/dianafranco

It was said that while blade fell from his hand in the last moment of his life he fell all the Guilt clashing onto him, shattering mind off to become the [[Guilt]] of the [[The House of Empty]] while his pale body expanded itself to feed on the blood left in the soil. 

In appearance he is the wall of flesh in the sealing chamber of [[Unmaker]] with a glowing eye looking at any that pass by him. 

Later on the followers of his would continue and create an [[Hedon Order]] with knowledge of the fate of the founding father forgotten.
