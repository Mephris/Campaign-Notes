## Avia, Aspect of Ambition

_Gargantuan Entity (Battlefield Hazard)_

---

**The Geometry of Silence** Avia is a stationary mandala of light and glass above the peak. She dictates where things are allowed to exist.
### Aspect Actions (The Clash)

_On Initiative count 20, Avia unleashes one of the following effects. All Squads and Players within 300ft must resolve the Save._

**_Absolute Zero (Flash Freeze)._** _Avia demands stillness. The temperature drops to the point where atoms stop moving._ :: **Clash Save:** DC 18 Constitution Save. :: **Failure:** 45 (10d8) Cold Damage + Unit's Speed is **0** next turn. :: **Success:** Half Damage. :: **Effect:** The ground becomes slippery ice. The **Sealing Gauge** moves -1 toward Sealing.

**_Gravity Well (Crushing Will)._** _Ambition creates weight. Avia increases the gravity of the mountain._ :: **Clash Save:** DC 18 Strength Save. :: **Failure:** 55 (10d10) Bludgeoning Damage + Unit is **Prone**. :: **Success:** Half Damage. :: **Effect:** Flying units fall immediately. The **Sealing Gauge** moves -1 toward Sealing.

**_Crystalline Cage (The Seal)._** _Glass walls erupt from the ground to compartmentalize the chaos._ :: **Clash Save:** DC 18 Dexterity Save. :: **Failure:** 33 (6d10) Force Damage + Unit is **Restrained** in crystal. :: **Success:** Half Damage. :: **Effect:** Creates walls of cover. The **Sealing Gauge** moves -1 toward Sealing. }}

## Dheriel, Unmortal Hero

_Gargantuan Entity (Battlefield Hazard)_

---
**The Glitch in the Ash** Dheriel does not move; she teleports between frames of reality. She is not fighting; she is editing the battlefield.
### Aspect Actions (The Clash)

_On Initiative count 10, Dheriel unleashes one of the following effects. All Squads and Players within 300ft must resolve the Save._

**_The Unmaker's Wake (Reality Slash)._** _Dheriel swings the white blade. The air screams as physics are violated._ :: **Clash Save:** DC 18 Dexterity Save. :: **Failure:** 45 (10d8) Force Damage. :: **Success:** Half Damage. :: **Effect:** Structures and Terrain in a straight line are erased. The **Sealing Gauge** moves +1 toward Unmaking.

**_Thermite Pulse (Nuclear Heart)._** _She vents the excess heat of the Firebird. The world turns white-hot._ :: **Clash Save:** DC 18 Constitution Save. :: **Failure:** 55 (10d10) Fire Damage + Unit is **Blinded**. :: **Success:** Half Damage + Not Blinded. :: **Effect:** Snow/Ice melts instantly. The **Sealing Gauge** moves +1 toward Unmaking.

**_Void Step (Reposition)._** _She ceases to exist here and exists there. The displacement creates a vacuum shockwave._ :: **Clash Save:** DC 18 Strength Save. :: **Failure:** 33 (6d10) Thunder Damage + Unit is Pulled 30ft toward the center. :: **Success:** Half Damage. :: **Effect:** Dheriel moves to any point.
