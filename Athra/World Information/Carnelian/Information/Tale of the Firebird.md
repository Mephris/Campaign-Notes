Based on the [[Tenets of Grahaim]] and many fables told by [[Saint Ilaine]]. Tale of the firebird is a set of stories representing the Carnelian History and its ages. 

At first it was a mere journal of the [[Saint Carnelian]] continued by [[Saint Ilaine]] and passed onto other incarnations of the [[Aspect of Conflict, Firebird]] until the time came of the 442nd year when the 10th generation saint awoken as [[Saint Archduke Solomon Grahaim]]
