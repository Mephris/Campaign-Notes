Fey are a race created by the [[Beauty]] in her illusionary song of pain.

As a race, they come in all shapes and sizes ruled by the Archfey as a counter to gods and lords of wonder.

They do not feel the sustenance from fruits and meat, instead taking it from the effort spent on an item.

On the outside of their body they have a pair of a body part, wings, antenna, or some sort of this depending on the fey appearance.

That body part is counted as their heart they must protect at all cost, for those are counted as the illusion spell made to create them. If amputated the body part remains alive, until fully destroyed. If any inteligent being takes hold of the wing, they are able to give commands to fey as if they were bound by contract. 


### fey culture 
Fae take pride in beauty, it might take weird forms but no place is as decorated and taken care of as their domain and their appearances.

Their culture is bound by the theatrics, tricks and flaire, while their combat evolved from their dance like nature.

Their clothing often shows the decor of wing like shapes with intricate patterns of bug like hexagon cells of different colours intertwining in gradient. Most of the times the clothes have a main outer part of wing like shapes, with folded fabric to keep them together and mobile. More often then not they posses marks upon their arms skin or long sleeves to dance on the wind especially when they use the flying metal disks during the war dancers attack.

Usually they avoid clothing and hairstyles that would touch their wings due to the uncomfortable feeling they get whenever anything touches them.
