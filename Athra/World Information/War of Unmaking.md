643 B.C (before Carnelian).

The War of Unmaking is the event making the beginning of the downfal of [[Bogóir]]. 

It was during this time that the [[The Unmaker]] fell upon [[Lethrai]]. In years following his fall he was wielded by the Bogóir who first wished destruction upon their prey dragons before turning the blade against themselves. 

After decades of self destructing war the last Bogóir to wield the blade, [[Hedon]], fell.
