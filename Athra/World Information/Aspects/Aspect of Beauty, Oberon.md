
The beauty that is both skin deep and deep within the heart, the Aspect of Beauty is took on its station in time of the [[Forgotten City]]. 

As a mortal, she was a songstress, a main entertaiment of the Arlekin Theatre within the slums of the [[Forgotten City]]. As an elf she was given a rare gift of innate illusion, her imagination paired with the music she created, releasing spells to bring the imagine canvas onto the outside visual and auditory experience. 

After a greatest show, she suffered a terrible burn due to the fall of the Arlekin Theatre.
