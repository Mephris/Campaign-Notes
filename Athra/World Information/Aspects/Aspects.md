The consciousness developed inside a domain and embedded onto a mortal soul. 

As the weapons of the old world fell their energies converged into large [[Spectral body|Spectral bodies]] that were defined by the [[Name (Age of Guidance)]] given to the weapon. Those bodies without a will to move them moved onto the new world, searching for a mortal that embodies its name. 

During the search the creatures slowly developed something, the longer they searched the more of the new ego was formed. 

The Oldest of Aspects take on will's of mortals, they are mortal souls given a purpose of keeping the [[Athra/Cosmology/Void/Void|Void]] creatures at bay. For all things that set their foot or soul in this new world will have to get by the godly weapons given will.
