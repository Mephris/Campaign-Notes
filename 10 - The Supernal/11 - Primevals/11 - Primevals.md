The Canvas and the Ink of Reality. The Foundation and all things which sprout from it. The [[11 - Primevals|primevals]] are the unthinking definitions of reality. What binds the world onto all which is perceivable and all which is experienced - good or bad. 

- [[Grael]], The Matter, Tyrant of Reality, all physical is part of their flesh.  
- [[Tuatha]], The Undefined, Liberator of Wills, their shifting flesh is all the meaning, all the souls, all the stains and possibility, all the stories written upon the physical which anchors it.
