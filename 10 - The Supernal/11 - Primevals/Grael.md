"I am that which Is, all the matter, all the law" 
![[Ishtar.png]]
[[Grael]] is the **Fabric**: the substance of the existence. She is the static, tangible and defined. Providing world with its structure. She of logic, indifferent rule, loom and the thread.  She is the logic that remains when the feeling fades.

**I. The Body of the World**
 - **The Substance:** She is the Matter - tangible bone of the earth, the roots, the heat, cold and energy. 
 - **The Rule:** She defined the internal logic of things; the "rule" that dictates how elements interact and bind together and intelligence. 
 - **The Phenomena:** She is the exposure, that which takes the defined physis and shows them onto the world by the phenomena. 

**II. Symbolism**
- **Eyes & Light** She is the tangible and shown, her symbolism is often representation of senses and uncovering light. 
- **The Sky** Azure skies with white clouds or mist on it represent the painted body of Grael. 
- **White** First creation of [[Grael]] was a quiet, pure reflective white - logical landscape that stood as a masterpiece of form before the [[Tuatha]] polluted it with imperfection and reason. 

**III. Ethical Stance**
- **Indifference** Grael is is absolute, but indifferent. It contains all logic. Whenever asked as to "Goddess why did you take my son?" she would answer "I didn't, but since he died because he worked in brimstone mines and had his skull crushed by the stone moved by the sudden influx of mana due to the nearby mining magic...."
- **Watcher** She is ultimate record keeper, holding all records of events upon the fabric that is her body. She does not offer no redemption or punishment - only the fact of the deed.

**IV. Note**
- [[Grael]] is highly intelligent being with low compassion and emotional understanding of [[Tuatha]]
- [[Grael]] always take a female form. 
- [[Grael]] in numerology is symbolized by number 3, which is also symbolizing "Stability/Knowledge". 
 - grael spoken in sentence or written in small letter is used interchangeably with a word's like body or matter
