"I am meaning, all the essence, the obscure"
![[Black Veil.png]]
[[Tuatha]] is the **Paint** or **Ink** upon the fabric: the significance of the existence. They are the dynamic, abstract and obscure. Providing world with its soul; meaning beyond just existence. That of emotion, abstract and the end.

If [[Grael]] is the parchment, [[Tuatha]] is the story written upon it. Without the ink, the page is empty; without the page, the ink is a puddle in the void. Tuatha is the whim of the heart—the beautiful, wasteful, and necessary tragedy of being alive.

**I. The Soul of the World**
 - **The Essence:** It is the soul - the personality, the emotion and the morality of being.
 - **The Nuance:** They defined the **Growth** and its unpredictability, all the non-logical impulses that give life its "flavor". 
 - **The Meaning:** They are the ink, that which flows down the fabric staining it with one individual color, the reason for existence. 
 - **The Stain** They are the shadow of the world, the hidden, the mark unfound and left behind by those forgotten from light of the present, if the soul dies and rebirths the mark of death is left as a stain. 

**II. Symbolism**
- **Veil & Darkness** It is the obscure, that which hides and keeps knowledge from all eyes, the unknown. 
- **The Nightsky** It is the dark skies which hold all the secrets and awe of the universe, the scars that show from under the blue dress(Daysky) of [[Grael]]
- **Black** Sacred color, which holds all other colors and from which all colors are derived. It was a black ink which [[Tuatha]] drank to sing the First Song. 

**III. Ethical Stance**
- **Compassion** Tuatha is undefined, together with its potential, it comes to all creation and can see their point of view; even if sometimes it shouldn't. 
- **Doer** She does not look upon the records, she simply acts according to a current whim of her being. 

**IV. Note**
- [[Tuatha]] is highly compassionate and emotional being without high understanding of her action. 
- [[Tuatha]] form is unpredictable and even her [[Aspects]] often take on more obscure forms.
- [[Tuatha]] in numerology is symbolized by number 4, which is also symbolizing "Chaos/Unpredictability"
