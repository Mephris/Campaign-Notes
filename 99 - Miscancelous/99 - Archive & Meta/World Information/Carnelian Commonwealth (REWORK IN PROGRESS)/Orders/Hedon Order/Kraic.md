##### Kraic

A short for Kryraic, a word from Bogóir tongue meaning ‘bloodrush’ - the primal satisfaction felt when hunting prey. Now describes the intense combat high that Hedon knights desperately chase through violence and cruelty.

_**GM Note**_ While technically a mercenary guild, these warriors revere their founder with religious fervor. Whether their superhuman tolerance for intoxicants and seemingly endless stamina stems from divine blessing or simple madness remains unclear.
