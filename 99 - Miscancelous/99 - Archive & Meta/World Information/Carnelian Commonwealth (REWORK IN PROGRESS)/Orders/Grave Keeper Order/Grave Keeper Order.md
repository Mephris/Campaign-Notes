# Clergy of Maransatti

_“...and upon the red fields of her slaughter, she seen the figures of beauty and death.  
To her, who painted fields red in sweet smell of rot, did beauty grant all the lifes she took.
So did the red knight smile with lips painted red of her kills. 
To her, ripped of morality, did Death grant a heart to understand the lives she took. 
and in this moment did the Grave Daughter fall her first tear, as all the field sang in her heart.
The beauty of eternal life, the suffering of her own sin, 
The first burial reprive of Maransatti.*"
