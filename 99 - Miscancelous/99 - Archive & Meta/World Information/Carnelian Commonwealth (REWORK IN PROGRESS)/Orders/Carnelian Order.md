### Order of Carnelian

_“If you think a blade will stop a Carnelian, you might want to look for a bigger one”_Captain Elrket

The Knights of Carnelian city stand as living symbols of human perseverance, renowned across the mainland for their unwavering resolve and martial excellence. Clad in distinctive tower plate armor, crafted with methods of cold smithing perfected through generations, these warriors rarely remove their steel shells except for treating wounds or performing sacred cleansing rituals. Their armor, forged in the ancient traditions of Carnelian’s master smiths, allows them to rest and even sleep while remaining battle-ready.

At the heart of their order lies a profound philosophy - life itself is an eternal cycle of conflict and rebirth. Each challenge faced is viewed as a battle, regardless of scale, and every outcome serves as a stepping stone toward their ultimate goal: the creation of the Crimson Jewel, a prophesied utopian paradise in the northern reaches.

What truly sets these knights apart is their indomitable spirit. Where others might falter after defeat, a Knight of Carnelian sees only another test of faith. This unshakeable resolve has earned humans both respect and fear throughout the realms, for what foe can triumph against warriors who view defeat merely as a temporary state between victories?

This legendary reputation is built upon a foundation of rigorous organization and profound personal sacrifice, creating a military structure that transforms individual devotion into collective might.

#### Organization and Training

Reformed by Archduke Solomon himself, the Order of Carnelian serves as the kingdom’s primary military force, handling all domestic problems and duties from guarding settlements to deploying patrol troops across the land. Their ranks are maintained with specialized troops rather than common soldiers, allowing humans to reach expert-level proficiency in three decades while still having decades of active service ahead of them.

Citizens who complete their foundational education at Draighearth Academy typically begin their service around age eighteen. Their previous incarnations’ talents guide their specialization - former war-smiths become siege engineers, past hunters become scouts, and those who died in battle often show natural aptitude for frontline combat.

During their first three years of battlefield deployment, squires gather deceased armors and weapons, serving not as soldiers yet but growing accustomed to the sounds and smells of warfare. Upon their 21st year, they are released from service to seek a Cold Smith who will forge their lifelong armor. It’s during these forging years that young squires form families and find homesteads to return to once they complete their military service. At times, it’s in these years that the most talented young knights choose to join other orders, particularly the elite Knights of Grahaim.

_**GM Note**_ Though many Knights of Carnelian find their initial faith in the Crimson Jewel tested by years of harsh service, they never truly abandon their sacred mission. Instead, their idealistic vision transforms into a more pragmatic devotion - each battle they wage, every hardship they endure, leaves an indelible mark upon the world. They find purpose in knowing their sacrifices pave the way for future generations, believing their own souls will be reborn to reap what they have sown. It is an unusual form of altruism, tinted with self-interest, yet noble in its acceptance of present suffering for future prosperity.

#### Territory

The Order maintains the largest and most prestigious district within Carnelian city, featuring expansive training grounds where the ring of steel echoes from dawn to dusk, heavily fortified armories storing centuries of accumulated weapons, and comfortable residential quarters that serve as temporary homes for knights between deployments. Their influence radiates throughout the kingdom via outposts and garrison towns, each serving as both defensive strongholds and recruiting centers for the next generation of Carnelian warriors.

At times they can be seen temporarily taking care of the abandoned territories of other Orders however more often then not those places quickly find their new owner before Carnelian Knights turn it into another outpost.

##### Carnelian Cold Smiths

In the city of Carnelian, smiths hold status equal to the knights themselves, for they are the keepers of sacred cold-forging techniques passed down through generations. Before taking their vows, aspiring knights spend years learning these ancient methods, crafting their own armor under master smiths’ guidance. This apprenticeship often becomes a bittersweet chapter in their lives - many form families with the smiths or their children, knowing they must eventually leave to fulfill their eternal duty. These knights return periodically to maintain their armor, using these visits to briefly reunite with the families they left behind.
