[[Spirits]] born of Ishtar and formed for specific purposes. Most of them are given a [[Name (Age of Guidance)]] specific to their station. The Guides travel the world in order to help mortals and nature.

While remaining inteligent they do not posses autonomy, their whole existence is bound by their gifts and they are made to find their goals fulfilling and at times even obsessive.

During the fall of [[Age of Guidance]] some guides survived the passage of the [[Cycle]] deep in the depths of [[The Nameless]].

The once cheerful guides made to help were warped and depraved onto becoming the [[Vacate]] ruling the [[The House of Empty]]
