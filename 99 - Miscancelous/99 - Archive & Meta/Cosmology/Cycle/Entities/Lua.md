The Betrayer and the Victor, Lua is the cosmic entity opposing the passage of the [[Cycle]]. Striving to stop the world from breaking apart while containing various [[Entropy]] with his meddling in the mortal world.

Once one of the [[Three Chamberlain's]], Champion granted a name "He who always rises equal to the challenge" or simply Edal.
He is the 7th Champion and the last one in the previous age.

Due to [[Entropy]] spreading rapidly and the Keeper and Scholar being slowly devoured by it, he was forced to raise the blade against his own [[Ishtar]]. Killing her and in turn kick-starting the passage of the [[Cycle]], causing the Ishtar to turn into a Nameless, and all the guides to be sealed within her hungering darkness.

Currently he resides on the Old World, spreading silvery light to shine onto the new world, guiding heroes and villains all in order to help the souls of those that passed from his own world.
