The shift of the cycle causes large amount of energy and rules of the world to shift from those of the newly depraved [[The Nameless]] onto those of a newborn [[Ishtar]].

During this shift many concepts are rewritten and in this moment sealed spells and items decay at rapid rate. It is during this time the empty world of Ishtar is filled with plant life and many of its denizens. 

There are examples of items or spells surviving the Cyclic Dispersion Phenomenon however that is attached to powerful mortals such as the [[Three Chamberlain's]] where the mortal power gains the [[Name (Age of Guidance)]]
