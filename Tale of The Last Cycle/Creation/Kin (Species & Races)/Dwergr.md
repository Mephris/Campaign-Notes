A dwarf is not a race, in fact they belong to another species all together and the word Dwarf simply represents their size among their kin. 

Dwarfs are created by [[Ambition]] during [[Age of Miracles - Life & Ambition]], by naming them with simple names that pushed them towards some paths in life rather then actual blessings. 

The first [[Dwergr]] gathered together to become a perfect embodiment of their [[Name]] such as "Smith" or "Hunter", but without ability to complete their goals... always too far high. They engraved names upon their bodies and blood so that their children and their children would complete their ambitions for them, resulting in short height among other disfigurements depending on the bloodline, in the end [[Dwergr]] became seen as members of another communities rather then other races, however it all depends on the Dwarven family.


# [[Dwergr]]
The folk as ambitious as they are honorable, [[Dwergr]] are a race of people appearing across all the other species. Often known for their short posture and at times small disfigurement in shapes of weird rune like birthmarks. 

They are a result of [[Ambition]]'s naming during the [[Age of Miracles - Life & Ambition|Age of Miracles]], the descendants of the [[Name|Named]] progenitors of the [[Dwergr]] clans.
