#### Magic
They are a culture most intwined with magic, using water magic to transport the hunted large prey and also to constantly keep their houses moving.
#### Housing
Each tribe has an elder that tends to the movement of house. The Household of a Tribe is a large hollowed from inside corpse of leviathan or another [[Sea King]]'s. 

Inside are kept many trinkets, leftover burial ornaments, utility stations. 
#### Burial 
Leviathans in nature are cannibalistic, but Mother Leviathan wanted a better fate for her children, so they no longer kill each other. Instead a bit of that culture persisted in the Burial rights. 

For both [[Mermaid - Valli]] and the prey they hunt they do same rite, using all parts of the hunted prey to make something, it is an obligation of a hunter and a family member to not let anything go to waste.
