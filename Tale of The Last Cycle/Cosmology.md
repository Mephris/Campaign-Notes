World of Athra is a conversation of meaning of life between [[Grael]] and [[Tuatha]]. 

Grael and Tuatha formed respectively the world of mortals bathed in light and spirit world bathed in darkness of Thuatha.

The world of living, is a sphere, with live being on the outer shell.
The world of the spirit envelops the world of the living, with the unlife and dreams being at the inside layer of the sphere.

Whenever Grael is not shining upon the skies, when the blinding light is gone, the skies can show the spirit world with it's many small lights lighten by the denizens of the spirit world.

The life and living world is like a river, and the spirit world is like a lake, it's waters stagnant so the dreaming fall into the lake like waterdrop rain to stir the stale waters.

Vampires and Werewolfs live in two world at same time, Vampires having to watch out for spirit body and physical, while werewolfs separating into a beast in the spirit realm and human in the physical.
