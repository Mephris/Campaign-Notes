## Story

### Age of Knowledge
#### Birth in Flames - Firebird
As the last breath of [[Wildern]] was taken from the "She-Owl" lungs, her feathers took flame and so did they spread through all the [[Grael]]. In this moment all creation slowly burned from within in flame, and in same moment as it all knew pain she was formed; The Firebird, the lady of suffering and growth.

On fiery wings she rose up onto skies, flying across all creation. Devouring all flame onto her large form. Her body forming onto a large ball of flames. In this moment she saw mortals spread from the flooded ink, creatures of all kind and size — but they were lone. Unlike the cradle where tribes had pained themselves into being, those emerging from the flood did not reach toward each other. Did not push. Did not clash. They simply existed, and existing was not enough.

So did she burn into their fabric the one thing she knew — motion. Not a reason. Not a destination. Just the fire that makes a creature turn toward another, toward the next day, toward anything at all. The refusal to be still.

Firebird born of the burning corpse of the [[Wildern|She-Owl]] 

The Patron of Eternal conflict who burns out in its passion within a single human lifetime just to be rekindled again. 

Feared by the enemies of [[Carnelian City]] the Carnelian Phenix is the symbol and the cornerstone of [[Carnelian Kingdom]].

It's incarnations with mortal life's and memories live within the [[Church of the Firebird]] as Saints of the faith, healing people and entering conflict only under Third Era's [[Nuclear Saint Doctrine]]
