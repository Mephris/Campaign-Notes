## Story
### Age of Knowledge
#### Birth in Horror
As the fiery tongue of [[Aelf#Tam-aelf|Tamwood]] torch licked the cheek of [[Beauty]]. The [[Tuatha]], the endless undefined stirred, responding to emotion of the end, of the great [[Empathy]] that she felt to the mortal creatures, and so from the burnt ink cloud formed a creature, that landed before the [[Beauty]].

His form was shifting, undefined as [[Tuatha]] herself, his image was dictated not by his shape, but by the face the onlooker most yearned to find. Obscure, he lowered himself onto a knee to touch upon the [[Beauty]]'s cheek, standing before her the consolation, her successor. As the [[Beauty]] slowly came to herself, the screams and pain came, and so he quickly came from the broken jagged loom. Staring upon the hell and pain of all creation all at once, and he saw that the dying souls simply dispersed onto nothing. 

In horror he watched as countless little worlds ended with the end of each soul, as the [[Beauty]] traveled to conclude her [[Age of Creation - Wildern & Beauty|Age]], he panicked, traveled the world of the cradle attempting to gather souls onto their body. Countless forms, memories, pains, yet it all echoes still, among them a single one louder then any other, who's rage dwindled to hollow pain. 

So did they meet, [[Empathy]] and the [[Defiance - Edal|Defiance]], in the last of the latter's moment, in this moment did [[Defiance - Edal|Edal]] extend his hand, planting a strand of fabric that once was his child onto what he seen as his lost love. In silence, did [[Empathy]] look as the he who stood victorious against two of [[Aspect]], denied his own victory, cleaving his form onto the cradle, ripping it from the face of the world. sending it to fall upwards to the skies. 

[[Empathy]] looked upwards as the first moon, and for a six days and six nights he looked upon the night skies, realizing that while day had sun and azure dress of [[Grael]], the night had naught but naked dark. 

He would change that, he would use the [[Defiance - Edal#Death|pain of Defiance]], its silvery aftermath as anchor, and make a home for all the dead, all the [[Tuatha]], high in place of the azure skies of day.
