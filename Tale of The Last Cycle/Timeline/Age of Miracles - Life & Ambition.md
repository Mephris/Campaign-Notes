## The Dawn of an Era
### Birth of [[Life]]
___
> [!abstract]
>  _Life emerges as a Grael Aspect — defined, feminine, muscleheaded and cheerful. Born with the single overwhelming compulsion to continue, to push forward, to get back up. She doesn't philosophize about it. She just does it. The world is new and loud and full of things to crash into and she is delighted._
> 
> _She and Ambition are introduced as the two centers of the age. They are immediately, obviously, going to be a problem together._

### Birth of [[Ambition]]
---
> [!abstract]
> _Ambition emerges self-contained and certain. He already knows what he wants. He already knows how to get it. He has no interest in conversation — not out of cruelty, just genuine conviction that everything he needs is already within himself. He looks at the world and sees potential to be shaped. He looks at Life and sees something that should be managed._

## Flourishing of an Age

### The Black Sea
---
> [!abstract]
> _When the world burned at the end of the Age of Creation, most of the ink evaporated — but the dregs sank. Down through the ocean floor into the deepest places, pooling into the Black Sea. Dark, lightless, cold. The ink of the old world preserved at the bottom of the new one._
> 
> _Ambition, unable to tolerate the idea of another Aspect being the center of the age, throws Life into the Black Sea._
> 
> _She climbs back out._

### The Battle of Hundred Years
---
> [!abstract] 
> _The first age where Aspects are in open conflict with each other. No subtlety, no proxy wars — direct opposition._
> 
> _Ambition works from above. He Names mortals with simple, functional names — "Best Smith", "Fastest Warrior" — not blessings but tools. Mental pushes that bend people toward his purposes. From these Named mortals, some begin to self-Name onto Grael flesh, attempting to pass their unfinished ambitions to their children. The first [[Dwergr]] bloodlines form from this — shortened, marked, innately driven by the unfinished will of dead ancestors._
> 
> _Life works from below — or tries to. Each time Ambition throws her into the Black Sea she claws back up, Names beasts with simple words — Live, Breed, Survive, Grow, Spread — and sends them into the world before getting thrown back down again. Her Names are thoughtless, functional, honest. The beasts shift and change with each new naming. Some become bipedal._
> 
> _A century or two of this. Life climbing, naming, getting thrown back. Ambition managing, naming, winning._

## Twilight of an Age
### Life Gives Up
---
> [!abstract]
> _Finally, Life stops climbing._
> 
> _Not dramatically. Not with a speech. She just... doesn't come back up. Lies at the bottom of the Black Sea in the dark, and for the first time in her existence feels no compulsion to rise. The beastly fight that defined her simply goes quiet._
> 
> _Mortal lifespans begin shortening across the world. Life no longer sees reason for life to go on, and the world reflects it._

### The Leviathan
---
> [!abstract]
> _At the bottom of the Black Sea, in the lightless dark, something is already there. The Leviathan — predator of the lightless nights, origin unknown, oldest thing in the deep. They meet._
> 
> _They speak. What passes between them is not recorded._
> 
> _But Life, for the first time, understands fate — that upon all the conflict, all the strife and all the happiness, the future of the world was always going somewhere. She relaxes. Not defeat. Acceptance._
> 
> _From this understanding [[Fate]] is born._
> 
> _And Life, still at the bottom, Names the creatures around her with something new — not Live or Survive but intelligence. The first [[Beastkin]] form from the deep._

### Ambition's Emptiness
---
> [!abstract]
> _Ambition looks down at the Black Sea. He won._
> 
> _He feels nothing._
> 
> _He travels the world — his world now, managed, shaped, running according to his will. Half the villages are dead from the shortening of lives. His Named mortals pursue their compulsions. The Dwergr bloodlines carve their ancestors' ambitions into their children's bones._
> 
> _He built exactly what he wanted. The emptiness does not make sense to him. He has no framework for it. He walks and walks and the world falls apart around him and he cannot understand why winning feels like this._

#### The Girl
---
> [!abstract]
> _He finds her in a dying village. A girl with less than a year to live, placing flowers on graves and hiding trinkets for people to find._
> 
> _He cannot understand her. She is losing. The world is losing. She is cheerful._
> 
> _He screams at her — WHY DO YOU GO ON?_
> 
> _She offers him a crown of flowers._
> 
> _"I may fall, but others will continue. I am laying down gifts to them, I am helping them so they smile."_
> 
> _Under the silvery moon, [[Hope]] is born. The crown of flowers lands on its head._
> 
> _The girl becomes the [[Aspect of Miracles]]._

### Aftermath

#### Fate & Life
---
> [!abstract]
> _Life does not come charging back up from the Black Sea. She rises slowly, changed. The brawler is still there — but now she shrugs at the things she cannot change. Accepts the shape of what is._
> 
> _"Oh well. That's life, I guess."_
> 
> _The world's lifespans begin returning to normal._

#### Ambition Opens a Door
---
> [!abstract]
> _Ambition does not know what to do with what he learned. He sits with it for a while — the crown of flowers, the emptiness of winning, the girl who gave away everything she had left and died smiling._
> 
> _He goes to [[Beauty|Danu]]._
> 
> _His first real conversation. He asks to borrow her [[Faekin]] — to weave illusions for the [[Dwergr]], to give peace to the progenitor spirits roaming the [[Spirit World]]. He is taking responsibility for what he built. Quietly. Without fanfare._
> 
> _He doesn't know how to talk to people yet. But he tries._
