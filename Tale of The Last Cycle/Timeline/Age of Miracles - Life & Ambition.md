### Birth of [[Ambition]]
---
As the Spirit World drowned in screams and laughs under the eye of [[Beauty|Danu]] and [[Empathy]], a newborn creation slipped them both. A young [[Aspect]], walking the [[Spirit World]] was [[Ambition]], the little spark in the darkness with his mind remembering the courts of [[Faekin]], the endless sky dress of [[Beauty|Danu]]. It had decided to make a robe of their own, to find people of its own, and set out to act.

During his travels, he found silvery colored strands and a dreamer, a weaver who wove beautiful world, so they spoke and for [[Ambition]] they wove a beautiful pale stainless robe.
*In return they asked for a name, for naught is free in this world, and they were named "The Best Weaver."*
[[Ambition]] held the robe to the light and was satisfied. [[Beauty|Danu]] had her sky-dress. Now he had something too.

So he traveled onward satisfied, and he found an old dreamer, a smith, and so he asked them to make him a beautiful ornate dagger. So he could protect himself and no longer just hide from the ferocious spirits.
*In return they asked for a name, for naught is free in this world, and they were named "The Best Smith."*
[[Ambition]] turned the dagger in his hand and felt safer. [[Empathy]] had his vast [[Nameless]] world to retreat to. Now he had something to protect himself too.

The newborn [[Aspect]] further traveled and protected himself, hiding when he could, attacking when he should, and he came across a great stone manor, and he seen a dreamer, stone worker, so he asked him to make him a manor of shadows. That would hide within the shadow of [[Ambition]].
*In return they asked for a name, for naught is free in this world, and they were named "The Best Mason."*
[[Ambition]] stood before his shadow manor and felt, for the first time, close to something. He had a robe. He had a blade. He had a home. He had things to show. He wondered if the other [[Aspect|Aspects]] would look at what he had built and finally see him standing among them.

Thus did the first goal come to its end. And young [[Ambition]] traveled the surface of the Spirit World, satisfied and bright.

One day, while gathering flowers for his brew, he met an old hunched dreamer sitting by the roadside. The dreamer looked at him — at his pale robe, his ornate dagger, his shadow manor trailing behind — and said nothing about any of it. Just held out a bouquet of beautiful neon violet flowers.

[[Ambition]] waited for the ask. The name. The price — but the dreamer stayed silent in the giving. So did he drink deep of the violet sweet brew, and fell into deep slumber with confidence of his deeds and happiness of his first gift.

He found himself stripped of all but the spark he began as. No more did he have the beauty of his robe, no more the protection of his dagger, no more the comfort of his home. In all the gifts gone he looked at himself, the insignificant spark, and sat in the dark for a long time, trying to think why he had forgotten.

_Naught is free in this World._
## Flourishing of an Age

### The Black Sea

> [!abstract] _When the world burned at the end of the [[Age of Creation - Wildern & Beauty|Age of Creation]], the surviving ink sank to the deepest places below the ocean floor. The Black Sea forms — lightless, cold, the dregs of the old world preserved in the new._
> 
> _[[Ambition]] throws [[Life]] into the Black Sea._
> 
> _She climbs back out._

### The Battle of Hundred Years

> [!abstract] _First age of open Aspect conflict._
> 
> _[[Ambition]] Names mortals — "Best Smith", "Fastest Warrior" — simple functional pushes used as tools. Some Named mortals begin Self-Naming onto [[Grael]] flesh to pass unfinished ambitions to children. First [[Dwergr]] bloodlines form._
> 
> _[[Life]] Names beasts from below — Live, Breed, Survive, Grow, Spread. Thrown back into the Black Sea each time she surfaces. Beasts shift and change with each new naming. Some become bipedal._
> 
> _This continues for a century or two._

## Twilight of an Age

### Life Gives Up

> [!abstract] _[[Life]] stops climbing. Lies at the bottom of the Black Sea. Does not come back up._
> 
> _Mortal lifespans begin shortening across the world._

### The Leviathan

> [!abstract] _At the bottom of the Black Sea, [[Life]] meets the [[Leviathan]] — predator of the lightless nights, oldest thing in the deep, origin unknown._
> 
> _They speak._
> 
> _[[Fate]] is born._
> 
> _[[Life]] Names the creatures around her with intelligence. The first [[Beastkin]] form from the deep._

### Ambition's Emptiness

> [!abstract] _[[Ambition]] looks down at the Black Sea. He won._
> 
> _He feels nothing._
> 
> _He walks the world. Half the villages are dead from the shortening of lives. He walks and cannot understand why._

### The Girl

> [!abstract] _[[Ambition]] finds a girl with less than a year to live, placing flowers on graves and hiding trinkets for people to find._
> 
> _He screams at her — WHY DO YOU GO ON?_
> 
> _She offers him a crown of flowers. "I may fall, but others will continue. I am laying down gifts to them, I am helping them so they smile."_
> 
> _Under the silvery moon, [[Hope]] is born. The crown of flowers lands on its head._
> 
> _The girl becomes the [[Aspect of Miracles]]._
