




Life and Ambition at odds with each other.

Black Sea - When world burned, most of the ink evaporated, but the bit that was left flew down to deepest bits of the world forming a Black Sea below the ocean. 

- Ambition not able to handle a thought of another [[Aspect]] being a center of the age throws [[Life]] down onto the Black Sea below the ocean. 
- Life rather then give up constantly fights and tries to get up no matter how many times it is stricken down, for live always is meant to put up the beastly fight, to survive and live. 
- The First Age where aspects are in open conflict happens, where Ambition forms tools to influence the age in form of mortals bearing simple [[Name]]'s. 
- Some of the mortals named by ambition begin to Self-Name themselves onto Grael, in order to pass their ambitions to their children. 
- Life at same time puts names onto beasts, telling them to grow, to spread, but in her lack of thought she does it by naming them simple things resulting in them "Live, Breed, Survive, Grow, Spread" constantly change and shift. 
- Life's Beast's are named different things each time Life manages to get onto land before being thrown again into the Black Sea. Resulting in some of her Beasts becoming Bipedal. 
- Century or Two passes like this, until Life finally... gives up and lays at the bottom of the Black Sea. 
- Ambition looks down upon the Black sea, he won, yet he felt no satisfaction. Just emptiness. 
- With Life defeated and down in the abyss, mortal life's grew shorter as Life herself knew no reason for Life to go on. This plague cut mortal life's short. At same time, she meets a Blind beast from the [[Age of Creation - Wildern & Beauty]], the Sea serpent. Leviathan, mother of all sea kings. Thus they spoke, and life for the first time realized the fate of the world, that upon all the conflict, all strife and all happiness the future of the world was fated, and so they relaxed and upon their creation they gave intelligence, forming the first [[Beastkin]]. Thus was the Fate born. 
- Ambition traveled the world falling apart, he went across a village of people and found half of them dead, as he traveled the world he found a girl, that would die in mere months, happily placing flowers upon graves and hiding trinkets among the village. He couldnt understand, why he who won felt empty and the girl of mere decade, was happy. And he screamed WHY?! WHY DO YOU GO ON?!, and the girl answered cheerfully while offering him a crown of flowers. "I may fall, but others will continue, I am laying down gifts to them, I am helping them so they smile" and thus ambition learned, that blind push onwards leads only to emptiness, and self-acceptance onto happiness. Thus in a miracle under the silvery moon he felt hope for the world, Thus was Hope born, and upon its head landed the crown of flowers as the girl became the Aspect of Miracles. 

so did the cycle continue until the life who always was one to never give up, to burn bright without stopping, he gave up, laying at the bottom for he saw no meaning to defying the fate. This was fate born.

And Ambition looked down and felt empty at it won, but it felt empty.
