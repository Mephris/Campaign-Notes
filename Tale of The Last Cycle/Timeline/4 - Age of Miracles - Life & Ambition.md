## The Dawn of an Era

### Birth of [[Life]]
---
> [!abstract] _[[Life]] emerges as a [[Grael]] Aspect. Feminine, cheerful, muscleheaded. Born with one compulsion — continue. The world is new and she crashes into it._

## Flourishing of an Age

### The Black Sea

> [!abstract] _When the world burned at the end of the [[2 - Age of Creation - Wildern & Beauty|Age of Creation]], the surviving ink sank to the deepest places below the ocean floor. The Black Sea forms — lightless, cold, the dregs of the old world preserved in the new._
> 
> _[[Ambition]] throws [[Life]] into the Black Sea._
> 
> _She climbs back out._

### The Battle of Hundred Years

> [!abstract] _First age of open Aspect conflict._
> 
> _[[Ambition]] Names mortals — "Best Smith", "Fastest Warrior" — simple functional pushes used as tools. Some Named mortals begin Self-Naming onto [[Grael]] flesh to pass unfinished ambitions to children. First [[Dwergr]] bloodlines form._
> 
> _[[Life]] Names beasts from below — Live, Breed, Survive, Grow, Spread. Thrown back into the Black Sea each time she surfaces. Beasts shift and change with each new naming. Some become bipedal._
> 
> _This continues for a century or two._

---

## Twilight of an Age

### Life Gives Up

> [!abstract] _[[Life]] stops climbing. Lies at the bottom of the Black Sea. Does not come back up._
> 
> _Mortal lifespans begin shortening across the world._

### The Leviathan

> [!abstract] _At the bottom of the Black Sea, [[Life]] meets the [[Leviathan]] — predator of the lightless nights, oldest thing in the deep, origin unknown._
> 
> _They speak._
> 
> _[[Fate]] is born._
> 
> _[[Life]] Names the creatures around her with intelligence. The first [[Beastkin]] form from the deep._

### Ambition's Emptiness

> [!abstract] _[[Ambition]] looks down at the Black Sea. He won._
> 
> _He feels nothing._
> 
> _He walks the world. Half the villages are dead from the shortening of lives. He walks and cannot understand why._

### The Girl

> [!abstract] _[[Ambition]] finds a girl with less than a year to live, placing flowers on graves and hiding trinkets for people to find._
> 
> _He screams at her — WHY DO YOU GO ON?_
> 
> _She offers him a crown of flowers. "I may fall, but others will continue. I am laying down gifts to them, I am helping them so they smile."_
> 
> _Under the silvery moon, [[Hope]] is born. The crown of flowers lands on its head._
> 
> _The girl becomes the [[Aspect of Miracles]]._

## Aftermath

### Fate & Life

> [!abstract] _[[Life]] rises slowly from the Black Sea. Changed._
> 
> _Mortal lifespans begin returning to normal._

### Ambition Opens a Door

> [!abstract] _[[Ambition]] goes to [[Beauty|Danu]]._
> 
> _His first real conversation. He asks to borrow her [[Faekin]] — to weave illusions for the [[Dwergr]], to give peace to the progenitor spirits roaming the [[Spirit World]]._

### The Aspect of Miracles

> [!abstract] _The girl who gave [[Ambition]] hope. Named only by what she became — [[Miracles]]._
> 
> _Connection to magic to be decided later._
