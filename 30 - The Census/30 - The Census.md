The mortals of the world are separated into two types of families, the:
- [[Ancestries (Species & Races)]] - Those are the naturally born creatures created by aspects, their are separated into species, which are separated into races. 
- [[Lineage]] - Lineage's are mortal's that placed a [[Name]] upon their flesh which makes them a separate race from their mother race yet they still have the same species, [[Lineage]]s are interspecies.
