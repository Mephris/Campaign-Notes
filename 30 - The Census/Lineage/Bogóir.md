Bogóir were the result of [[Dakr - Human|Dakr]] ingenuity during the [[3 - Age of Miracles - Life & Ambition|Age of War]]. 

When [[Valka]] marches against [[Dakr - Human|Dakr]] it was the creation of [[Bogóir]] that stopped and pushed back her march. Ever since the [[Valka|Mother of Drakes]] died and no more giant enemies encroached their numbers diminished in favor of... harder to hit infantry. 

Their settlements still can be found around [[Lethrai]]
