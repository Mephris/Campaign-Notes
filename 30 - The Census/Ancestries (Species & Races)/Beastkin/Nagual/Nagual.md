The [[Nagual]] are the races of [[Beastkin]] that remained bound by their instinct, throwing away or being untouched by the gift of intelligence. They remain somewhat intelligent but they are always at odds with their instincts. 

The [[Nagual]] often are considered more of beast race then [[Beastkin]], even though they are intelligent. They often remain beastlike, form packs or are territorial and recluse.

## Word Building
> From aztec word describing a "Light between stars", the shadow, the cold, the night, the shifting. A concept very similiar to Tuatha. 
> In the setting its used to describe beings that came in some way from the Primordial ink of Danu.
