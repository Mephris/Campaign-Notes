Beasts that began in the flooded nights of the [[1 - Age of Creation - Wildern & Beauty]]. 

Three has been counted to survive and thrive, all of them ending as [[Totems]] of [[Life]]. 

Valli - Mother of Coiled Ones ([[Wyrm]])
Valka - Mother of Armored Ones ([[Drake]])
Vani - Mother of the Bespoken Ones ([[Wyvern]])

Their descendant beasts, [[Wyrm]], [[Drake]] & [[Wyvern]] are [[Dragons]] however they do not posses the might of their totem, usually a word "Dragon" is a revered title pointing to the 3 Mothers. 

> [!note] Nagual & Dragons
> Dragons are older then the category of Nagual. Meaning that not all of the original [[Dragons]] were Nagual, but after the [[Life]] naming, they became ones.
