### Min - Minaelf
> **Scottish Gaelic** word _mìn_ (pronounced /miːɲ/), inherited from **Old Irish** _mín_.
> > **Proto-Celtic:** Derived from ***mīnos**.
> > **Proto-Indo-European (PIE) Core:** Likely from the root ***mey-** (“small,” “thin,” or “soft”).
> > **Original Meaning:** "Smooth," "fine," "delicate," "soft," or "gentle."

In the [[1 - Age of Creation - Wildern & Beauty|Age of Creation]], [[Wildern]] took upon herself to purify her creations, the peaceful animals eating the wicked black roots were took onto the loom and reformed onto Min. 

Min are slender humanoids around 4-6(1.52 m - 1.83m) feet tall with sharp ended ears, the remainder of their beastly ancestor, and lightly skinned colors living mainly in the northern mountains.
