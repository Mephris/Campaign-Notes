The Second of the [[Dragons]] that was named by [[Life]]. After the fall of life onto sea by hands of [[Valka]], [[Vani]] was deeply injured and recceeded onto the servitude to [[Valka]] as one of [[Totems]]. 

She was the great beast that kept the packs of [[Valka]] protected with her children, the numerous beasts that roared powerfully as both signals and marks of territory.
