Children of [[Valka]] before her demise, surviving mainly due to their number during the [[3 - Age of Miracles - Life & Ambition|Third Age]].

Drakes are in short, wingless dragons, their forms focusing on thick armor of sharp scales, evolved from being a prey to larger predators of the [[1 - Age of Creation - Wildern & Beauty|First Age]] ink predators. Their eyes were result of hunts made during the day. 

These days among the creatures of Primordial Ink they are slowly dwindling in number, unless up north where the [[Valka]] ice clan has been born.
